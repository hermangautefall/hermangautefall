Herman Gautefall

Pianist, Composer and Educator // Oslo, Norway

"I play jazz because it's the only music that is never finished."

Jazz pianist, film composer and music teacher from Kristiansand — based in Oslo. From classical orchestra to jazz stages in Norway, Italy and Greece.

Herman Gautefall Olsson is a jazz pianist, film composer and founder of Løkken Music School in Oslo. Trained at the University of Agder under Jan Gunnar Hoff and with an exchange year at Saint Louis College of Music in Rome, he has built a career spanning solo evenings and jazz festivals to film music for international productions. He performs with artists such as Sofie Fjellvang, Celine Berstad and The Daisy Sisters, and has appeared on stages from Kongsberg Jazz Festival to the Kardamili International Jazz Festival in Greece.
