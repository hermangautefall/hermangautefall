Piano lessons tailored to you

I have over seven years of experience as a piano teacher from private and municipal music schools, and a master's degree in rhythmic music from the University of Agder. I work daily as a performing pianist and composer, and that shapes the way I teach.

I teach all styles and genres — classical music, pop, rock, jazz, blues, R&B, film music and electronic music. You decide what you want to learn, and we find the way there together.

As my student you get:

  • ✓ Weekly one-on-one piano lessons of 30 minutes
  • ✓ Tuition adapted to your level, from beginner to advanced
  • ✓ Genre of your choice: classical, jazz, pop, folk, film music and more
  • ✓ Practice plan and tips between lessons
  • ✓ Access to acoustic piano in soundproofed rooms centrally in Oslo

Who are the lessons for?

Children and teenagers (from approx. 6 years)

Children learn to read music, technique and musical understanding through a programme that balances mastery and enjoyment. Teaching is adapted to the child's age and interests, in a safe and motivating learning environment.

Adult beginners

It's never too late to start playing piano. Many adults begin from scratch and quickly experience the joy of mastering their first pieces. Adults often have a clear idea of what they want to learn — making lessons efficient from the very first session.

Advanced pianists

Want to develop your improvisation, technique or repertoire further? I help you with targeted practice and new challenges suited to your level and ambitions.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need my own piano at home?

Having access to a piano or digital piano at home for practice between lessons is an advantage, but not an absolute requirement to get started. I'm happy to advise on good alternatives in different price ranges.

How old do children need to be to start?

Most children can start from around age 6. The most important thing is that the child can sit focused for short periods and has a desire to learn.

What genres do you teach?

I teach all genres — classical, jazz, pop, rock, folk, film music and more. Lessons are always tailored to what you're passionate about.

Why do you teach through Løkken Music School?

Løkken Music School is a private music school I have established in Oslo, with several skilled teachers. This gives students stable tuition, cover arrangements in case of illness, and a professional pedagogical offering. You can read more at lokkenmusikkskole.com.
